# How to Set Up IPTV on Your Firestick: The Complete 2026 Guide

The Amazon Firestick is hands-down the most popular device for streaming. Its sleek interface, portability, and powerful hardware make it the perfect match for a premium IPTV subscription like **RedStream™**. 

If you just bought a subscription or a free trial and want to get it running in under 10 minutes, this step-by-step tutorial is for you.

---

## Step 1: Enable Apps from Unknown Sources

Because premium IPTV players are not always hosted directly on the official Amazon Appstore, we first need to configure our Firestick to allow side-loaded apps.

1. Turn on your Firestick and navigate to the **Settings** menu (the gear icon on the far right).
2. Click on **My Fire TV** (or **Device & Software** on newer models).
3. Select **Developer Options**.
   * *Note: If you don't see Developer Options, go to **About**, highlight your Fire TV device name, and click the center button on your remote 7 times to unlock it.*
4. Turn on **Developer Options** and set **Apps from Unknown Sources** to **ON**.

---

## Step 2: Install the Downloader App

We will use a secure utility called **Downloader** to safely download our IPTV players.

1. Go back to the Firestick Home Screen and click on the **Find** or **Search** button.
2. Type in `Downloader` and search.
3. Click on the orange **Downloader** icon and select **Download** to install it.
4. Launch Downloader and select **Allow** when prompted for storage permissions.

---

## Step 3: Install a Premium IPTV Player (e.g., IPTV Smarters Pro)

We highly recommend **IPTV Smarters Pro** or **XCIPTV** for their modern layouts, high-performance engines, and multi-language support.

![IPTV Smarters Pro Setup](/1000148220-iptv-france.webp)

1. Open the **Downloader** app.
2. In the URL search box, type the following direct download code for IPTV Smarters Pro: `74221` (or enter the URL: `https://www.iptvsmarters.com/smarters.apk`).
3. Click **Go** and wait for the file to download.
4. Once downloaded, click **Install** and then click **Done**.
5. You can choose to delete the APK installation file to save space on your Firestick!

---

## Step 4: Configure Your RedStream™ Credentials

Now that your player is installed, it’s time to connect to our high-performance server.

![RedStream Premium Channels Interface](/1000148226-iptv-france.webp)

1. Open **IPTV Smarters Pro** on your Firestick.
2. Select **Login with Xtream Codes API** (this is much faster and more stable than standard M3U playlists).
3. Enter your login details provided by your **RedStream™** support agent:
   * **Name:** `RedStream`
   * **Username:** *[Your Username]*
   * **Password:** *[Your Password]*
   * **Host URL:** *[Provided Server Host Link]*
4. Click **ADD USER** and wait 30 seconds for the channel playlist, VOD movies, and electronic program guide (EPG) to fetch.
